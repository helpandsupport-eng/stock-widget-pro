FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Still have questions? Contact support →

How do I add NSE or BSE stocks?

Append .NS for NSE (e.g. RELIANCE.NS) or .BO for BSE (e.g. TATASTEEL.BO). You can also start typing the company name in the search bar — the autocomplete will suggest the right symbol. NSE & BSE are Pro-only features.

Does the extension work offline?

Yes — prices are cached locally. When you open the popup offline, you'll see the last known price with a "stale" indicator. Prices refresh automatically when your connection returns.

How does the Pro licence work?

Pay once via UPI/bank transfer and send your transaction reference (UTR). We generate a unique licence key tied to your email and UTR. Enter the key in the extension — no account needed, works on any reinstall.

What happens if I reinstall the extension?

Your watchlist and Pro licence are stored in Chrome's local storage and are wiped on reinstall. Use the Backup button before removing the extension to save a copy, then restore it after reinstall.

Where does the price data come from?

Prices are fetched directly from Yahoo Finance's public API — the same data used by finance.yahoo.com. No intermediary server is involved.

Is my data sent anywhere?

No. Stock Widget Pro has no backend server, no analytics, and no account system. The only network requests are to Yahoo Finance for price data. Everything else stays in your browser.

Can I use the extension on multiple computers?

Yes. Simply activate your Pro key on each browser using the same email and UTR you submitted when purchasing. The key is device-independent at activation.
