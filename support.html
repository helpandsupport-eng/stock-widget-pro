🔧 Troubleshooting Steps
1

Symbol showing "Not found"

Check the suffix. Use RELIANCE.NS for NSE, TATASTEEL.BO for BSE, HSBA.L for LSE, BTC-USD for crypto. US stocks have no suffix (e.g. AAPL). Indices use ^ prefix (e.g. ^NSEI).

2

Prices are stale / not updating

Click the symbol in your watchlist to force a refresh. If you have Pro, check your auto-refresh setting. Outside market hours, prices reflect the last close — this is normal.

3

Pro not activating after entering key

Make sure you're using the exact email and UTR (transaction reference) you submitted in your payment request. Both fields are case-sensitive. Copy them from your original payment confirmation message.

4

Lost Pro licence after reinstalling

Chrome wipes local storage when an extension is removed. Always use 💾 Backup (in the quote card footer) before uninstalling. To recover without a backup, email us with your original UTR and email — we'll regenerate your key.

5

Extension not appearing / not loading

Go to chrome://extensions, make sure Stock Widget Pro is enabled. Try clicking the puzzle-piece icon in the toolbar and pinning the extension. If it still fails, remove and re-add from the Chrome Web Store.

💬 Frequently Asked Questions
General
How do I add a stock to my watchlist?

Type the symbol in the search bar and press Add or hit Enter. Start typing a company name (e.g. "Reliance") and the autocomplete will suggest the right symbol. Click a suggestion to fill it in.

What symbols are supported?

All symbols supported by Yahoo Finance are supported. Common formats:

  • US stocks: AAPL, MSFT, TSLA
  • NSE (India): RELIANCE.NS, TCS.NS
  • BSE (India): TATASTEEL.BO, SBIN.BO
  • LSE (UK): HSBA.L, BP.L
  • Xetra (Germany): SAP.DE, SIE.DE
  • Crypto: BTC-USD, ETH-USD
  • Indices: ^NSEI, ^GSPC, ^DJI
Does it work outside market hours?

Yes. Outside market hours the extension shows the last closing price with the market state badge (CLOSED / PRE-MARKET / AFTER-HRS). Prices refresh automatically when the market opens.

Where does the data come from?

Price data is fetched directly from Yahoo Finance's public API — the same source used by finance.yahoo.com. No intermediary or proprietary server is involved.

Pro Licence
How do I purchase Pro?

Click ⭐ Upgrade to Pro inside the extension (or the Upgrade button in the header). Pay ₹99 via UPI/bank transfer, enter the UTR (transaction reference number) from your payment app, and click Send Payment Proof. We'll email you a licence key within a few hours.

How do I activate my Pro key?

Open the extension → click Upgrade → scroll to the Already have a key? section. Enter the email you used in your payment request, your UTR, and the key exactly as provided. Click Activate Pro.

Can I use my licence on multiple computers?

Yes. Activate with the same email and UTR on each device. The key binds to the device at activation time and works independently on each browser.

I reinstalled and lost Pro — what do I do?

If you have a backup file, use Restore Backup in the Upgrade page to re-activate. If not, email us at helpandsupport.eng@gmail.com with your original payment UTR — we'll send your key again.

I get "Key does not match this email, UTR, or device"

The key is generated from the exact email and UTR you submitted. Common causes: extra spaces, wrong email (check your payment confirmation), or incorrect UTR. Copy-paste all three values directly from the email we sent. If it still fails, contact us.
