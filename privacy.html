Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 July 2025  ·  Last updated: 1 July 2025

📋 Short version: Stock Widget Pro collects no personal data whatsoever. Everything stays in your own browser. No accounts, no servers, no tracking.

1. Who we are

Stock Widget Pro is a Chrome browser extension developed and maintained by helpandsupport-eng. If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at mithunvm92@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect

We collect no personal data. The table below lists every category of data the Chrome Web Store asks about:

3. What is stored locally

The extension stores the following data in chrome.storage.local — this is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Watchlist — the list of stock symbols you add (e.g. AAPL, RELIANCE.NS)
  • Cached price data — last fetched price, change, volume etc. for each symbol, used to display prices when offline or between refreshes
  • Auto-refresh preference — your chosen refresh interval (off / 30s / 1m / 5m)
  • Pro licence data — licence key, email address you submitted for Pro activation, and UTR reference, stored locally to validate your Pro status on each load. This data never leaves your device.

4. Network requests

The extension makes HTTPS requests to Yahoo Finance's public API only:

  • https://query1.finance.yahoo.com/
  • https://query2.finance.yahoo.com/

These requests contain only the stock symbol(s) as URL parameters (e.g. ?symbols=AAPL) to fetch publicly available price data. No user data, identifiers, or personal information are included in these requests. These requests are made directly from your browser to Yahoo Finance — they do not pass through any server operated by us.

5. Third-party services

The extension uses Yahoo Finance as its sole data source. Yahoo Finance's own privacy policy applies to the requests made to their API. We have no control over Yahoo Finance's data practices. You can review their policy at legal.yahoo.com.

We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, Sentry, or any other analytics, crash reporting, or advertising service.

6. Pro licence & payment

Pro licences are purchased via direct UPI/bank transfer. Payment processing is handled entirely by your bank or UPI app — we have no access to your financial account details, card numbers, or bank credentials. After payment, you provide your UTR (transaction reference number) to us via email. We use the UTR to generate your licence key. The UTR is stored locally in your browser solely for licence validation. It is not shared with any third party.

7. Children's privacy

Stock Widget Pro does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. As no data is collected at all, this extension is safe for all age groups.

8. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and update the extension's Chrome Web Store listing. Continued use of the extension after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
